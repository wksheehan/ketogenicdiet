Butter Coffee or Tea

1. Brew two cups of your favorite Coffee or Tea.
2. Pour into a blender
3. Add 2 tbsp of grass fed butter to the blender
4. Add 1 tbsp of MCT Oil to the blender
5. Blend it up for about 20 seconds and you're ready to go!
*Note: Play around with the amount of MCT Oil--I recommend starting small and building up to around 1-1.5 tbsp
