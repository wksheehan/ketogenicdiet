My Tips

Breakfast

  • Butter Coffee or Tea
  • That's it! Have this every morning and you should be feeling full of energy and not even hungry for about 4 hours! Plus, you won't feel jittery or experience the normal crash you typically get with coffee.

    Lunch

    Eggs, bacon, and avocado. Cook the eggs in about a tablespoon of butter or coconut oil. Make sure to add plenty of salt.

    Dinner

    Salad with feta cheese, olive oil, avocado, and fatty chicken

    How to Make:

    Butter Coffee or Tea

    1. Brew two cups of your favorite Coffee or Tea.
    2. Pour into a blender
    3. Add 2 tbsp of grass fed butter to the blender
    4. Add 1 tbsp of MCT Oil to the blender
    5. Blend it up for about 20 seconds and you're ready to go!
    *Note: Play around with the amount of MCT Oil--I recommend starting small and building up to around 1-1.5 tbsp
