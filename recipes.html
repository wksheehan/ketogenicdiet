Butter Coffee or Tea
That's it! You should be feeling full of energy and not even hungry for about 4 hours! And, you won't have a normal crash typically associated with coffee.

Butter Coffee or Tea

1. Brew two cups of your favorite Coffee or Tea.
2. Pour into a blender
3. Add 2 tbsp of grass fed butter to the blender
4. Add 1 tbsp of MCT Oil to the blender
5. Blend it up and serve hot or over ice!

Note: You can play around with the amount of MCT Oil--I recommend starting small and building up to 1-1.5 tbsp
